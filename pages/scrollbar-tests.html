Scroll Container Patterns

Hover the items, then toggle content length to compare shift behavior across the three patterns.

A. overflow-y: auto
Default. Hover paints to the right edge when no bar; toggling content length shifts the layout when the bar appears or disappears.
B. scrollbar-gutter: stable
Current rule. Never shifts, but the hover background is visibly cut off where the (often invisible) gutter sits.
C. overflow-y: scroll + tinted track
Proposed. Never shifts. The rail is always rendered, so the hover cut-off aligns with a visible scrollbar rather than empty space.
